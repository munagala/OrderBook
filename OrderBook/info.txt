
OrderBook Functionality :
	1.  Accept and add both bid and ask orders to book.

	2.  Match the orders against available bid/ask orders.

	3.  When bis/ask orders match with available orders in book, then intimate trader with success message.
	    Refer to tradeLog.txt for filled order information.

	4.  Pending order information will be displayed in terminal. 

	5.  OrderBook is programmed with most suitable data structures like associative containers for better latency.
